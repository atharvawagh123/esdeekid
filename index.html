The Story

WHO
IS EsDeeKid

Born and raised in Liverpool, EsDeeKid emerged from the city's underground scene with a sound that cuts through the noise. His delivery is raw — thick Scouse accent, atmospheric production, words that hit like concrete.

Debut album Rebel (2025) marks the arrival of a distinct voice in UK rap. No polish. No compromise. Just cloud rap with edges sharp enough to draw blood.

This is Liverpool energy. This is Rebel.
